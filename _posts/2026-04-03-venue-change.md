Due to some recent vandalism of the annex space, Pastimes will unfortunately no longer be able to host our event. However, we were able to find a different LGS in the Chicagoland area who stepped up to help us make this work as best as possible. 

This means Squad Goals 2026 will now be hosted at Second City Games! 
https://secondcitygames.com/
1350 Old Skokie Valley Rd, Highland Park, IL 60035
(Second City games is a BYOB venue)

One important change with this venue is that as it's a newer store, it has limited space for tables at this time. This means that the cap for Squad Goals will be 18 teams total, which coincidentally is the number of tickets we've sold so far. So if you've already gotten your ticket, you're still in the event and good to go. But if you don't think you'll be able to make it, please let us know as soon as possible so we can try and find a replacement. 

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out [https://www.squadgoalsmcp.com/contact/](https://www.squadgoalsmcp.com/contact/)
